Our Journey

Get LO is founded by a group of 5 students in the Singapore Management University.

It all started as Samuel is working as a waiter at Stabrucks. Samuel realises that a lot of beverage is thrown away if it is wrongly brewed. Cakes and pies is also thrown away if it's not purchased every night.

Samuel started to wonder if a website to sell still-good condition food of hawker centres, cafe or even restaurants to others would be possible. He managed to gather a group of his friends and started working on it.

In November 2021, the website is created! Today, we continue to press on with our mission to zero food waste!

Our Mission

Eliminate food waste and build a better food system for everyone

The problem of food waste needs no introduction. In 2020, an eye popping 665,000 tonnes of food waste is generated. This accounts for about 11 per cent of the total waste generated in Singapore. Various methods of managing waste (eg. disposal, donation etc), meanwhile, have their own unique challenges.

Get LO provides an alternative solution that will help address food wastage, while providing value to merchants and everyday consumers.
